Food Guide

Patsy's Pizzeria

Address: 61 W 74th St, New York, NY 10023

Patsy's Pizzeria is a legendary pizzeria in New York City, known for its classic thin-crust pizza. Established in 1933, it has become a favorite among locals and tourists alike. The restaurant's rich history and delicious pies make it a must-visit for pizza lovers. Stop by here after exploring Central Park with your family to end off a perfect day.

Hooda Halal

Address: 601 W 115th St, New York, NY 10025

When coming to New York City, you must try Hooda Halal, a popular food truck known for its delicious halal dishes. From flavorful chicken and lamb over rice to mouthwatering gyros, Hooda Halal offers a taste of authentic Middle Eastern cuisine. This spot is a favorite among locals, along with college student who flock to it for a quick and satisfying meal.

Noa, A Café

Address: 34 E 32nd St, New York, NY 10016

Noa, A Café is a charming café located in the heart of New York City, offering a cozy atmosphere and a diverse menu. From artisanal coffee to delectable pastries and light bites, Noa provides a perfect spot for a quick breakfast or a leisurely lunch. Its inviting ambiance makes it an ideal place to relax and enjoy a meal while exploring the city. Make sure to try their soft cinnamon rolls and their signature matcha lattes, which are a hit among regulars.
