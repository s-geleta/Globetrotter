Top Attractions

Staten Island Ferry

The Staten Island Ferry offers a free, scenic ride between Manhattan and Staten Island, providing stunning views of the Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, and the New York Harbor. It's a popular way for visitors to experience the city skyline and waterfront.

Central Park

Central Park is an expansive urban park in the heart of Manhattan, offering a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and bustle. Visitors can enjoy walking paths, lakes, gardens, and various recreational activities, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and exploration.

Roosevelt Island Tramway

The Roosevelt Island Tramway is an aerial tramway that connects Roosevelt Island to Manhattan, offering a unique and scenic view of the East River and the city skyline. It's a fun and convenient way to travel while enjoying breathtaking views. Make sure to check it out during sunset or night for a magical experience.
